CUPSLY

Cupsly is a luxury people-first brand committed to redefining the eco-friendly reusable coffee cup. At the intersection of innovation and fashion-forward design, Cupsly champions craft and sustainability, meticulously curated to meet the desires of our community. ​Our mission is to craft a luxury reusable coffee cup, elevating it into a symbol of fashion. We aim to mirror our values of artistry, timeless innovation, and an exclusive community, inspiring visionary ideas and self-expression, transforming each sip into a statement. ​At Cupsly, we envision a world where luxury, fashion and sustainability intersect.

The Story of Cupsly so far.

The Idea

It all began in 2022 while standing in my local coffee shop. I've always been a passionate coffee drinker but always left without a non-reusable coffee cup. Why? There wasn't a reusable cup that suited my style and aesthetic. Something that was more premium and looked good. So, I decided to create my own statement piece. This was the moment I founded Cupsly. Think of it more like a fashion accessory. Something you would be proud to use. Something you would feel inspired by. Something you would look good with. Your aesthetic matched.

The Making of the Statement

Transition

After I realised this problem, I reached out to some friends to ask what they thought. While some loved it and some hated it, the more people I asked, the more positive feedback I received. This gave me the confidence to go all in. I met with branding agencies and product designers to create a minimum viable product (MVP). During this process, I met Rachael, a friend who had built one of the biggest media agencies in the world. I asked her to help me market this project as I wanted to craft a premium product that would attract the right people. The more we planned the marketing, the more I wanted her to come on board. After six months of conversations, she became my co-founder.
